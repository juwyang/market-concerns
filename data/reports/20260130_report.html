  • **Monetary Policy Risk:** The nomination of a hawkish Fed Chair (Keven Warsh) introduces uncertainty and potential for a stronger dollar and higher rates, pressuring assets like precious metals.
  • **Weather Dependency:** Natural gas and coffee prices are highly sensitive to immediate weather patterns (US cold, Brazil rain), which can change rapidly.
  • **Geopolitical Swing Factor:** Crude oil prices demonstrated sensitivity to shifts in rhetoric regarding Middle East tensions, highlighting ongoing volatility risk.
