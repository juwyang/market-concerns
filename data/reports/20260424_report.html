Key Themes

  • **Geopolitical optimism dominates:** Hopes for US-Iran peace talks drove the dollar lower, crude oil and gasoline down, and supported risk appetite.
  • **Soft commodities mixed on supply/demand:** Sugar rose on short covering and real strength, while cocoa and coffee fell on demand/supply concerns.
  • **Grains show no clear trend:** Wheat profit-taking, soybeans modest strength, corn mixed – news and social media headlines (Article 7) appear to influence trading more than fundamentals.

Risks Highlighted

  • **Abundant natural gas supplies:** Persistent oversupply and mild spring weather continue to pressure gas prices (Article 11).
