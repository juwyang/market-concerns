Risks Highlighted

  • Geopolitical escalation in the Middle East could further disrupt crude oil supply or lead to rapid price swings.
  • Beneficial weather for cocoa (dry conditions) may reverse if the pattern changes, causing disease concerns to return.
  • Surplus fears in sugar could intensify if the Brazilian real continues to weaken or if consumer preferences shift further.
  • Profit-taking or a lack of follow-through after the grain bounce could reverse the short-term gains.

Watch Next

  • Further developments in US-Iran hostilities and any ceasefire or escalation signals.
  • US weather forecasts for hotter temperatures (impacting natural gas and grains).
  • El Niño confirmation – further crop weather implications for coffee and other softs.
  • USDA reports or cash trade updates for livestock and grains.
