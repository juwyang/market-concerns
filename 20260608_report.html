Key Themes

  • Commodity markets were mixed on Monday, with several sectors showing divergent moves.
  • **Energy & Geopolitics:** Crude oil edged higher due to renewed Middle East tensions, while natural gas tumbled on milder US weather forecasts.
  • **Agriculture:** Grains were broadly mixed with winter wheat providing support, but soybeans and some livestock (hogs, cattle) faced weakness. The cattle market was impacted by a confirmed screwworm case.

  • A weaker Brazilian real continues to be a bearish driver for sugar prices.
  • A second confirmed case of screwworm is a risk for the cattle supply chain.
