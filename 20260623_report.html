Key Themes

  • **Broad Commodity Weakness:** Many commodities (wheat, cotton, cattle, crude oil) faced selling pressure on Tuesday, partly influenced by a stronger US dollar and falling equity markets.

  • **Weather Disruptions:** While currently supportive for coffee and cocoa, disruptions to harvests and seasonal demand patterns create volatility.
